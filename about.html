About Me

I'm a Graphic and Multimedia Design student, currently based in Madrid, after three years studying in Barcelona. I'm passionate about building and communicating through visual design — from shaping brand identities to crafting editorial pieces.

I'm looking for new opportunities to keep learning, expand my vision and explore different creative fields while growing professionally and personally. Alongside my studies, I've developed a strong interest in editorial design and layout, color theory, and typography. Beyond digital work, I enjoy craft-related projects and handmade layouts, which help me stay connected to the tactile side of design.
